What's this ?

Nuxeo UX Lab

This prototype shows a simplified and easy to use browsing view, to let you imagine how work and collaborate readily. Browse and give us your feedback about this new navigation by filling this 4 minutes survey to help us continue to improve our product & your experience.

Note that lots of features are not implemented yet: you won't be able to create a document or to resolve a task for real.
Simplified document view

The tree is folded by default, tabs are on the left side, we display less element to not disturb your attention.

Access the tree from everywhere

From a document, from the dashboard, when browsing tables. In the case you need it, it's in the corner.

Create your file when you want

After a search or in the dashboard, if you think about a document to create, create it directly from where you are. Note that the actual document creation is disabled in this stateless demo

Sticky toolbar for documents

The sticky toolbar keeps document actions handy: no need for scroll to top anymore.

Side panel for shortcuts

Open the right panel to see your tasks, your favorites, your collections and more. No need to have another step to access your documents.

Edit your document from their location

On a table listing, hover the row to directly interact with your document.

Personal Workspace in Home

The personal workspace was hard to locate. Now it's simply in your home tab.

Personal Settings under the user menu

Your personal settings have moved from the Home tab to the user menu below your name.
